Some Of The Best Movies Of All Time

These are all timers (In no particular order)

1. Hot Fuzz

A seriously genius film. This is film 2 out of The Cornetto Trilogy and definitely the best. The easter eggs are fantastic, the foreshadowing throughout the film is rather genius, and it gets better with every watch.

2. Happy Gilmore

Easily the best Adam Sandler film. I watched this many times as a kid and still find it just as funny as an adult today. I simply yearn to be able to drive the ball like Happy. RIP Chubbs.

3. Superbad

Superbad is a great embodiment of youth and rebellion (despite me doing very little in my teenage years) and it is both equally heartwarming and hilarious. I could have picked any title for third place but it was mainly between this and Mohammed (read a book for once).
